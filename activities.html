activities

A sailing holiday is not just about spending the time in the water or on board the yacht, however comfortable. It is also about seeing new places, often inaccessible by land, trying something new, having a new holiday every day. During your charter and depending on your sailing route we will be able to offer the following activities:

sightseeing

A sailing holiday will let you see different places by definition. Your cruise can be designed to include the most unique and interesting anchor points in your itinerary. But you may also wish to explore the history and customs of the country you are visiting a bit more – and we can offer the tours you will enjoy and remember – from small villages and mud baths to ancient towns and Roman heritage.

diving

A specialist with all the necessary equipment will be waiting for you in a pre-arranged spot to help you explore the best underwater locations along your cruise.

bike tours

Enjoy the breath-taking seaside scenery from hilltops and the coast as well as from the water. You can explore established cycling routes and visit local villages, combining fun and good fresh air exercise. For your safety and comfort, a minivan with first aid kit and other supplies will be following you.

hiking trips

We will suggest the best spots on shore to explore on foot. Fill your morning or afternoon with walking and exploring new places, and make some of your most memorable photos before returning to the comfort of the Queen and refreshments awaiting for you onboard.

horse riding

Whether you’d like to enjoy a ride or take a horse riding lesson, we will try to fit this exciting activity in your schedule.

yoga classes

There are professional yoga teachers who can instruct you during your holiday. The classes can be situated in idyllic isolated bays offering quiet and serenity in outstanding natural beauty.

spa & wellness

We can start with a visit to Hamam (Turkish bath) or local spas on shore and continue with massages and beauty treatments onboard. We can invite a qualified massage and beauty therapist to spend with you as much time as you desire.
